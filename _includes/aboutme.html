About Me

I'm an ASTRO 3D postdoctoral fellow at the Australian National University's Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics, collaborating with Lisa Kewley.


I completed my PhD at the University of Washington, Seattle, under the supervision of Julianne Dalcanton. While at UW, I was involved with Astronomy on Tap Seattle, the UW mobile planetarium and the Pre-Major in Astronomy Program (Pre-MAP).


When I'm not engaged in more scholarly pursuits, I can be found gardening or building guitar pedals. I love tabletop games and beer, and firmly believe that the two should be enjoyed together.
