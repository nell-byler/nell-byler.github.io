About Me

I'm currently a Senior Data Scientist in artificial intelligence at the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory in Seattle.

Previously, I was an ASTRO 3D post-doctoral researcher at the Australian National University's Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics. I have a PhD in astronomy from the University of Washington, Seattle. While at UW, I was involved with Astronomy on Tap Seattle, the UW mobile planetarium and the Pre-Major in Astronomy Program (Pre-MAP).


When I'm not engaged in more scholarly pursuits, I can be found gardening or building guitar pedals. I love tabletop games and beer, and firmly believe that the two should be enjoyed together.
