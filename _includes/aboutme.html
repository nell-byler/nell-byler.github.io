About Me

I'm currently a graduate student at the University of Washington in Seattle. I'm generally interested in galaxy evolution and using nearby galaxies to better understand observations of more distant galaxies.


As of January, 2018, I'll be relocating to Canberra, Australia to start a post-doctoral fellowship at the Australian National University.


I help run Astronomy on Tap Seattle, and am involved with the UW mobile planetarium and the Pre-Major in Astronomy Program (Pre-MAP).


When I'm not engaged in more scholarly pursuits, I can be found gardening or building guitar pedals. I love tabletop games and beer, and firmly believe that the two should be enjoyed together.
